A new step into the webs future

A new home for your browser, free without account

What?

This page is designed for desktop monitors to provide you with a better browsing experience. It serves as a fully customizable, user-friendly start page. Some of its features include a search bar searching with your preferred search engine, notes in order to jot down ideas, the current date, and fun facts to make your day a bit more interesting and informative, not just handy but enjoyable.

How?

Set this page as the default start page of your browser, and you are good to go. More on that can be found by clicking on "Get going" Then it should open automatically every time you open up your browser, with all its features and adventages. Access the web through your favorite search engine, write notes or to-do lists, and start your browsing session with a chunk of information in an appealing format. Everything now is right here on your start page.

Why?

We believe that in a world of information overload, the ability to browse the web without distractions becomes more and more important. This page is designed to do just that, so as to make your workflow better for anything that needs to be done. Whether you're doing research, taking notes, or just casually browsing, this will give you the tools to do it all in one convenient place.
